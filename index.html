Welcome

Thank you for visiting Sociovoices! This is a space for meaningful conversations uncovering diverse perspectives with Sociology PhDs.

Our Vision

SocioVoices is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to creating inclusive spaces where diverse voices and lived experiences are heard, shared, and valued. We envision a society where storytelling becomes a powerful tool for connection, healing and change—one in which everyone, regardless of background, feels seen and empowered to speak.

Our Mission

We use storytelling to spark understanding, challenge inequity, and build collective strength. SocioVoices promotes inclusive dialogue that centers underrepresented perspectives—especially those often left out of mainstream conversations. At the heart of our work are the stories of individuals who, in their own unique ways, strive for a better life while making meaningful contributions to their communities and society. We believe in the power of real voices to shift narratives, foster empathy, and drive meaningful action toward a more just and equitable society.

Our Defining Values

  • Authentic Storytelling
  • Equity and Belonging
  • Collaboration and Co-Creation
  • Compassionate Listening
  • Interconnectedness and Inclusion

欢迎

感谢您访问Sociovoices！这是一个与社会学博士一起探索多元观点、进行有意义对话的空间。

我们的愿景

Sociovoices 是一个非营利组织，致力于创造包容性的空间，让多元声音和真实经历被倾听、分享和重视。我们希望通过讲述故事，成为连接、疗愈和改变的有力工具，让每个人无论背景如何，都能被看见并有勇气发声。

我们的使命

我们通过讲述故事激发理解、挑战不平等、凝聚集体力量。Sociovoices 推动以包容为核心的对话，关注主流话语中常被忽视的群体。我们的核心是那些以独特方式追求更好生活、为社区和社会做出贡献的真实故事。我们相信真实的声音能够改变叙事、促进共情，并推动社会迈向更加公正与平等。

我们的核心价值观

  • 真实讲述
  • 公平与归属
  • 协作与共创
  • 同理倾听
  • 互联与包容
